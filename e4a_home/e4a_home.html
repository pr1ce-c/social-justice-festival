about us


Equity for All(e4A) is a local grassroots organization focusing on uplifting people of color throughout the San Francisco Bay Area who have been unjustly subject to discriminatory legislation. Comprised of members from and/or based across the Bay Area, we’ve come together to help our communities, establishing e4A as a proactive coalition providing resources for any legal, financial, and medical inquiries.



event overview


Equity for All is hosting a three-day conference focused on identifying and combating instances of racial inequity that are found throughout the Bay Area. Held in Oakland’s revered Fox Theatre, this event will provide a variety of interactive informational sessions curated with the intention to educate and empower individuals who wish to contribute to the cause.
